CREW DETAILS

Meet the Hestia crew

John Walker

Mission Commander


Age: 25

Origin: United Kingdom

Background: Strong scientific background in astrophysics with proven leadership experience. He has led research teams in remote locations and has a deep understanding of the challenges involved in space exploration. His blend of scientific knowledge and leadership skills makes him perfectly suited to command the Hestia mission.

Tessa Carrington

Mission Specialist


Age: 30

Origin: United States

Background: Aerospace engineer with a focus on spacecraft systems, previously worked in NASA mission planning. Tessa was instrumental in designing the HAB's systems and plays a key role in maintaining its functionality.

Robyn Martinez

Regologist


Age: 28

Origin: United States

Background: Grew up in the American Southwest and studied Martian analog sites. Robyn's work involves researching the Martian landscape and geological features. She's passionate about planetary exploration and plays a crucial role in field research.

Harrison Blake

Systems Operator


Age: 32

Origin: United Kingdom

Background: Coming from a family of mechanics, Harrison has vast experience working on the ISS and is skilled in spacecraft systems. He is responsible for ensuring that all mechanical systems on Mars remain operational and secure.

Larisa Volkov

Medical Officer


Age: 34

Origin: Russia

Background: A trauma surgeon who has worked in combat zones and disaster relief, Larisa is now focused on space medicine. She is responsible for the health and well-being of the crew, conducting medical research, and handling emergencies.

Eli Foster

Communications Specialist

Age: 27

Origin: Australia

Background: Former radio astronomer and satellite technician, Eli specializes in maintaining communication with Earth and managing the crew's data and research transmissions. Eli is responsible for overseeing data collection and supporting scientific research.

Sofia Alvarez

Botanist & Life Support Specialist


Age: 29

Origin: Spain

Background: Sofia grew up on a vineyard in Spain and later studied astrobotany at MIT. She is focused on food sustainability on Mars and works on growing plants in the habitat to support the crew's life support systems.

Adam Reyes

Logistics Officer



Age: 31

Origin: United States

Background: A former special forces operative, Adam is responsible for security and crisis management on the station. He handles logistical support, ensures the safety of the crew, and responds to emergencies, both within the HAB and on missions.

Mira Jónsdóttir

Astrobiologist



Age: 26

Origin: Iceland

Background: Mira is an expert in extremophiles and has studied life in extreme environments like deep-sea vents and deserts. She is highly motivated to find evidence of Martian life and is a key player in astrobiological research on the mission.
