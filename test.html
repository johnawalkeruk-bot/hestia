Astrobiology Analysis - Valles Marineris

Submitted by: Mira Jónsdóttir, Astrobiologist

Report #: 2025-AR-0225 | Version: 1.2 | Local Time: Sol 135, 14:27 MST

Summary

The mission team conducted an astrobiology field analysis. The main objective was to study potential biosignatures in regolith layers. The sample collection took place near the western ridge of Valles Marineris.

Findings

Initial analysis shows carbon compounds and minerals indicative of prebiotic chemistry. The samples suggest the presence of an ancient hydrothermal system.

Analysis Methodology

The analysis involved spectroscopy, mass spectrometry, and microscopic examination of the samples to identify any microbial patterns or organic compounds.
